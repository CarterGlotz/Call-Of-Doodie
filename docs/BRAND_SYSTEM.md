# Brand System

## Brand promise

- core promise: a sharp, ridiculous, replayable comedy shooter that feels great to control
- audience perception target: hilarious, fast, surprising, and far more polished than a throwaway parody

## Voice

- sounds like: confident, punchy, playful, self-aware, irreverent
- does not sound like: grim military fiction, cynical internet sludge, generic mobile-ad voice

## Visual direction

- core motifs: exaggerated weapon names, readable chaos, bright arcade feedback, absurd enemy personalities, run-defining celebratory effects
- avoid: muddy realism, borrowed military branding, overdesigned clutter, lifeless placeholder presentation

## Naming rules

- weapon naming: goofy, memorable, punchy, joke-forward
- enemy naming: personality first, easy to remember, clear enough to discuss in strategy talk
- feature naming: should feel playful without becoming confusing

## Approval gates

- what requires brand review: title changes, icon direction, major UI tone shifts, store copy, social voice, and anything that weakens the comedy-first identity
